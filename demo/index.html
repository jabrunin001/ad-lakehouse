Medallion in motion

Raw events in. Trustworthy tables out.

Real event streams carry duplicates and late arrivals. Bronze keeps everything raw and append-only; silver deduplicates on event_id and lands late events in their true partition; gold reshapes that into analyst-ready products. Send a burst and watch the layers do their job.

Privacy as an operational cost

Delete one user out of millions — and prove it.

GDPR fines run to 4% of global revenue, so "we can't actually delete this person" reaches the board. The data is laid out so a user lives in one predictable slice of it, instead of scattered across every file.

15× less data rewritten per deletion than a naive layout, measured on the demo — plus an audit record of what was erased and when.

Open table format

Point any engine at one copy of the data

Storage, catalog, and compute stay separate on Apache Iceberg. Trino queries the exact tables Spark writes — no proprietary warehouse holding your data hostage, no re-copying to swap engines.
